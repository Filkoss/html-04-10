David Pastrnak

David Pastrňák (* May 25, 1996 Havířov), nickname click to view is a Czech professional ice hockey forward currently, playing for the Boston Bruins in the American NHL. On June 27, 2014, he was drafted in the first round of the 2014 draft as 25th overall by the Boston Bruins.

He started his hockey career in his native Havířov. He took his first hockey steps at the Havířov ice stadium in September 1999, where his mother Marcela brought him when he was three years old.[2] Pastrňák started playing hockey in girls' figure skates, but even with them he mastered the necessary basics of skating. When he was six years old, his hockey skills were already at such a level that even as a preschooler he played matches for the 1st, 2nd, and even the 3rd grade. Later, at the age of 15, he also played for the A team of the Havířov club.
